Brief Summary:
Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Jilin University, under the supervision of Prof. Tao Peng. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree from the College of Computer Science and Technology at Jilin University in 2018 (GPA 3.66, top 2%), and studied for two years as a Master student. I am mainly interested in Natural Language Processing (NLP), especially Information Extraction, Aspect-Opinion Pair Extraction in Sentiment Analysis, and Pre-trained Language Models.
